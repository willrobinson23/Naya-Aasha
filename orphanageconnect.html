About SOS Children's Village

SOS Children's Villages in Bharatpur, Nepal

SOS Children's Village Bharatpur opened its gates in August 2003 and was officially inaugurated in November 2003. Up to 140 children can find a new home in its 14 family houses. In addition, there is the village director's house, a community centre, co-workers' houses as well as the necessary administration buildings.
